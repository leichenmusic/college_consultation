# **Invitation to Open Vocal Master Class in China**

**Organizer:** Beijing International Music Exchange
**Time:** Around Christmas 2025 (Exact dates to be finalized)
**Location:** Beijing, China
**Host:** Partner institutions in Beijing, with support from local music organizations and conservatories
**Language:** English (with translation support provided)

---

## **Overview**

We are pleased to invite distinguished vocal professors and artists from the United States to conduct **open master classes** in Beijing during the 2025 Christmas season. This event is part of a cross-cultural series designed to strengthen artistic exchange between the U.S. and China, bringing world-class vocal pedagogy and performance insight to young Chinese singers.

The master class will follow the successful format of recent international collaborations, such as the **2025 Venice Conservatory Vocal Master Class** led by Prof. Silvia Dalla Benetta, which attracted professional singers, university students, and passionate vocal enthusiasts across China.

---

## **Target Participants**

The invited students will include:

1. University and conservatory voice majors
2. Opera singers and professionals in vocal performance
3. Voice teachers and instructors
4. Students preparing for overseas study in vocal arts
5. Amateur singers with strong musical interest

---

## **Class Format and Activities**

Each master class is 45 minutes per student, providing one-on-one instruction and repertoire coaching. The session may include:

* Vocal technique development tailored to voice type
* Artistic interpretation and stage performance guidance
* Repertoire coaching on diction, musical phrasing, and character study
* Open interaction and Q&A with students
* Optional participation in closing recital or concert

In addition to the teaching sessions, we encourage informal exchanges with local teachers and students, fostering long-term connections between U.S. and Chinese vocal communities.

---

## **Logistics and Support**

* **Travel & Lodging:** Round-trip transportation within China and **room & board** in Beijing will be provided by the organizers.
* **Facilities:** Professional rehearsal and performance venues equipped with piano accompaniment and translation assistance.
* **Schedule:** Typically 3–5 days of classes, plus one public concert or showcase.
* **Publicity:** The event will be promoted across major Chinese conservatories, music academies, and social media platforms to ensure visibility and engagement.

---

## **Next Steps**

We warmly welcome interested professors and artists to express their availability for this collaboration. Once confirmed, we will share detailed schedules, class formats, and local coordination information.

We believe this exchange will not only enrich the local musical landscape but also create a meaningful cultural bridge between the United States and China.
